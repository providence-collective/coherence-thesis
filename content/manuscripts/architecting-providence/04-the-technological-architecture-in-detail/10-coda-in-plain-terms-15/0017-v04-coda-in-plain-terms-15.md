The plain point: AI here is plumbing, not a king. What it is forbidden to do is the real design. — At the Cardinal Scale the system remembers, surfaces, and suggests — and never decides. The people decide, slowly, in rooms. When residents worry about the technology, the answer is always the same: it cannot govern you, by construction. That short sentence is doing enormous work, and this chapter is why.


**Toward the coherent substrate.** Left to the extractive order, intelligence this powerful becomes the most efficient harvester yet devised. Bound as infrastructure under human governance — forbidden to rule — it becomes a tool of the pivot instead of its undoing. What AI becomes is decided by the substrate that governs it, not by the AI.
