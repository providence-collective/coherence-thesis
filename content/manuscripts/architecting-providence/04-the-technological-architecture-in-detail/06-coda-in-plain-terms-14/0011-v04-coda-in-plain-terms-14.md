Plainly: the power of a network is letting trust travel — so a stranger far away can rely on you — without a platform owning that trust and taxing it. — When the Cardinal Scale vouched for a young apprentice to a workshop three hundred miles away, no background-check company sat in the middle. Two communities trusted the same protocol, and that was enough. This chapter is how trust moves without a toll booth.


**Toward the coherent substrate.** Extraction inserts itself as the middleman and taxes the trust that passes through. Trust that travels on a shared protocol, owned by no one, is coherence's connective tissue — what lets strangers and communities rely on one another by their word, rather than through a rent-taking platform.
