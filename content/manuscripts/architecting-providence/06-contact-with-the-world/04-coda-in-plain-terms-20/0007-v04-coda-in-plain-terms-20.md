Plainly: the danger is not only failure — it is success, which invites the world to buy you, copy you hollow, or bend you toward what it already understands. — When the Cardinal Scale drew attention, it drew offers: to franchise, to partner, to scale fast on someone else's money and someone else's terms. Saying no to good-looking offers turned out to be a core skill. This chapter is how to see the co-optation coming.


**Toward the coherent substrate.** The extractive world does not ignore what threatens it; it tries to absorb, copy, or bend it back. Surviving contact is keeping the pivot from being quietly pulled home. We claim no immunity here — only that anyone moving us off extraction had better understand the pull before it arrives.
