The second address insisted that you are small but not nothing, and that no curve of death was ever bent by one pair of hands alone. Here, then, is not a program but a *stone* — concrete, modest, layable inside seven days. None of it is the whole roof. All of it is the roof. Choose one. Then, next week, choose another.
